What is your interest in IT?

I'm interested in IT because it has been a huge influence in my life, through various forms such as video games and computers. I've been very fond of these since a young age, as the easiest way to make a friend was to invite someone over to play video games. I always had a particular interest in robotics, as well as the coding that goes on behind the scenes. There has been a huge boom in the field of IT recently, as correlated with advances in technology in general. As such, many new jobs and opportunities will come into existence.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

Given that I had no previous background in IT, I took the time to research the different options for IT study, considering the entry pathways, topics of study, and support networks. I also talked to friends who had attended RMIT in the past, and they gave fairly promising reviews, highlighting the student support and quality of lecture/tutorial content.

I came to the conclusion that this was a very suitable university for me, for several reasons:

- it was a place where I would get the most out of my studies,

- I could really immerse myself into the work and get support if and when I needed it

- The locations of the university meant it was easily accessible through public transport, and

- The student support services like RUSU compass.
